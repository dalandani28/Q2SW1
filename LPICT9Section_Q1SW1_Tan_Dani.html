Noli me Tangere Characters

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra)
A student from Europe(San Diego,) who returned home in the Philippines seven years later. He is oftenly described as an idealistic person. Returned to his hometown to establish a school, to end up learning about his father's death.
Maria Clara (de los Santos)
Fiancee of Cristosome Ibarra, as well as a kindhearted, high social standing woman. A supposed daughter of Captain Tiago, until later on it was discovered that Padre Damaso is her biological father who had an affair with Captian Tiago's wife.
Padre(Priest/Father) Damaso
Oftenly described as arrogant and brash. The former town curate of San Diego, and is against the marriage of Ibarra and Maria Clara. He put Filpino's name in vain as he describes them as Indios, and insult the natives.
Elias
A selfelss, mysterious man who grew up wealthy, who soon learned his family's history and lost everything. He was obliged to help those who are being opressed by the society. He despises the Spanish colonial government and Catholic churches besides from the fact he is religious.
Captian Tiago(Don Sanitago de los Santos)
A busninessman in San Diego. He is the one who welcomed Ibarra after him studying in Europe. A wealthy Filipino, who keeps ties with higher-ranking members of the Catholic Church. Not having enough to confidence to speak up, he recklessly joined in on the insults being thrown to his (natives) name.
